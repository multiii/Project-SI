About Us

About a year and a half ago, I was determined to start taking things seriously. My dream was to improve myself as a person and develop myself into the individual I wanted to be. Having learned programming a few years prior solely through online tutorials, I knew how fulfilling and easy it actually is to develop new skills and abilities when you know what you're doing, even if you think it's impossible.

After reading many books, learning from my experiences, breaking through my self-defined boundaries, making major progress as an individual, and finally becoming whom I truly wished to be, I had learned how rewarding and exhilarating the journey of self-improvement is. It feels like you're in the best era of your life, and like everyday is a surprise that you can't wait for once you get to bed.

I started Life Transform to guide others towards this same treasure. It fills with me pride and happiness to say that I can help others feel the same happiness and unlock the same feelings of being on top of the world that I felt and still feel, by the help of my knowledge and experiences. My goal with this project is to give back to the world and help others join me at the peak of life's mountain, since it geniunely feels a little lonely at the top, and I wish more people would make it to where I have come.
